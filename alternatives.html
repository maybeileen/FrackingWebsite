The Solution

Alternative sources of of energy, such as solar power and hydro power will replace, or lessen the need for coal and oil. In addition, to reduce the impact that fracking has on water supply, developing a more efficient water treatment technology will be more economical and reduce fracking fluid.

What we can do

Get involved. Join community and state petitions or council meetings where regulatory codes and laws are being revised. Make yourself heard, and educate others on the dangers of fracking.

Another solution can be to implement equipment designed to reduce the emission of pollutants, or more specifically, methane.

Ultimately, these alternatives will not fully stop fracking but lessen the detrimental effect it has on our society.
